History

Wikipedia was launched in 2001 as a free, web-based, collaborative, multilingual encyclopedia project supported by the Wikimedia Foundation. It is one of the largest reference websites on the internet.

Features

Wikipedia uses collaborative editing, allowing millions of people worldwide to contribute articles. It is powered by the MediaWiki software.

Impact

Wikipedia has profoundly impacted the way people access and share knowledge, offering free access to information across multiple languages.
